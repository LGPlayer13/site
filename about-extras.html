EXTRA INFO ABOUT ME

Music? Well, my favorite artists are Tame Impala, MGMT, Beck, Bill Wurtz, Vampire Weekend, and Foster The People. I also like The Strokes, Gorillaz, bo en, GUM, Arctic Monkeys, and Tally Hall. If you'd like to recommend music for me to check out, feel free to in the Guestbook.

Favorite books? What are you talking about? I don't read, unless you count Diary of a Wimpy Kid.

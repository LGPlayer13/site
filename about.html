ABOUT ME

Good morning. I'm LG, some male kid (actually, I'm 15, believe it or not). I like to make small games on Scratch and IGM, play dumb video games, and rant. This is my web site, where I vent about my struggles, talk about my games, and unravel the odd story of the discs.

I'll be updating this web site very frequently, as I have a lot more to rant about and a lot more stories to tell. Hope you all enjoy what I have to offer, unless I'm not offering anything at the moment.

If you're here to get in contact with me, you can do that via my Discord server, found below the Navbar. Otherwise, have fun on my digital home on the world wide web.

ABOUT THIS SITE

This web site was made as a way for me to talk about the stuff I talk to myself about without having to use a platform like X (formerly known as Twitter). I also get to add all sorts of fun details everywhere.

The reason it doesn't look nearly as fancy as most modern websites is because I'm pretty mid when it comes to HTML and CSS, and I'm actively still getting used to their quirks.

Oh yea, this website used to be hosted on Neocities. However, the CSS took forever to upload (it still does...?), and the file size was limited (albeit I'd probably never reach the limit).

INFO ABOUT ME

Good morning. I'm LG (he/him), some kid who likes to make bad Scratch and IGM games in my freetime, often trying to balance gaming, game-making, and school.

My proudest works include George 1 Remastered and The Lost Labyrinths, found in the Games page.

Now, what do I like? Well, I like Minecraft. I play it way, way too much. I also really like the movies Holes, Coraline, James and the Giant Peach, and Charlie and the Chocolate Factory.

Music? Well, my favorite artists are Tame Impala, MGMT, Beck, Bill Wurtz, GUM, Gorillaz, Arctic Monkeys, Tally Hall, and The Strokes. Also, I don't like rap and country music.

INFO ABOUT THIS SITE

This web site was made as a way for me to talk about the stuff I talk to myself about without having to make a million Scratch projects about it. I also get to add all sorts of fun details everywhere.

The reason it doesn't look nearly as fancy as most modern websites is because I'm pretty mid when it comes to HTML and CSS, and I'm actively still getting used to their quirks.

Oh yea, this website used to be hosted on Neocities. However, the CSS took forever to upload (it still does...?), and the file size was limited (albeit I'd probably never reach the limit).

Also GitHub is just better

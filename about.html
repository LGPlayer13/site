INFO ABOUT ME

Good morning. I'm LGPlayer13 (aka LG). I'm some kid who likes to make bad Scratch and IGM games in my freetime, often trying to balance gaming, game-making, and school.

My proudest works include George 1 Remastered and The Lost Labyrinths, both on Scratch and IGM respectively. You can find those in the Games page.

Now, what do I like? Well, I like Minecraft. I play it way, way too much. I also really like the movies Holes, Coraline, and Charlie and the Chocolate Factory.

Music? Well, my favorite artists are Tame Impala, Gorillaz, MGMT, Arctic Monkeys, Tally Hall, and Bill Wurtz.
